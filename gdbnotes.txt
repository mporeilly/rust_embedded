(n)ext - Steps through a single line of code. Steps over function calls.
(ni) - Steps through a single x86 instruction. Steps over calls.
(s)tep - Steps through a single line of code. Steps into function calls.
(si) - Steps through a single x86 instruction. Steps into calls.
